Our Story

Traditional Fish & Chip Shop with a difference

Fish Shack is a lively new Fish & Chip Take Away situated in Havengore, Chelmsford, Essex.

A traditional 'chippy' menu with a difference, our primary focus however is serving the tastiest fresh, sustainable fish, hand sourced from Billingsgate Market. Choose from either crisp fried in batter, grilled or coated in breadcrumb served alongside fresh cut chips, lemon wedge & homemade tartare sauce.

In addition to fantastic Fish & Chips our selection of fresh Cold Counter fish & seafood favourites including delicious Fish Platters along with selection of Handmade Pies, Butcher's Meat, Kids Meals Boxes complete with Toy, Vegetarian & Vegan Options, Desserts, 'Catch of The Day' Specials & fantastic Daily Offers ensures there is something for everyone at Fish Shack.

Open 7 days a week from 8am, you will be welcomed by our warm friendly team for our Breakfast, Lunch or Dinner Take Away here at Fish Shack.
